About Me

Dedicated and accomplished IT professional with a Master's in Computer Application. I have a strong background in both education and industry, with expertise in C#, Java, and MySQL, as well as data mining and database management.

My academic journey includes research in data mining, culminating in a thesis on the evaluation of classification algorithms for predicting student performance. This experience sparked a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence and its applications in education and beyond.

Currently, I am pursuing my M.Phil. in ICT, further expanding my knowledge and research capabilities in the field. Throughout my career, I have served as a lecturer, program coordinator, and Head of Department, demonstrating exceptional leadership, program development, and coordination skills. I am particularly adept at designing and implementing innovative courses that meet industry standards, engaging students, and delivering effective curriculum.

I have also demonstrated proficiency in using analytical tools such as WEKA and RapidMiner. I am a registered engineer with the Nepal Engineering Council. Additionally, I am known for my ability to manage classrooms effectively, provide differentiated instruction, and integrate technology into teaching.
